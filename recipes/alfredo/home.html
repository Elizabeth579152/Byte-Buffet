Pancake Recipe

Ingredients Instructions
1. 1 cup all-purpose flour, (spooned and leveled) 1. In a small bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt; set aside.
2. 2 tablespoons sugar 2. In a medium bowl, whisk together milk, butter (or oil), and egg.
3. 2 teaspoons baking powder 3. Add dry ingredients to milk mixture; whisk until just moistened (do not overmix; a few small lumps are fine).
4. ½ teaspoon salt 4. Heat a large skillet (nonstick or cast-iron) or griddle over medium. Add ½ tsp of butter.
5. 1 cup milk 5. For each pancake, spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons of batter onto skillet, using the back of the spoon to spread batter into a round.
6. 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil 6. Cook until surface of pancakes have some bubbles and a few have burst, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip carefully with a thin spatula, and cook until browned on the underside, 1 to 2 minutes more.
7. 1 large egg 7. Serve warm with maple syrup, strawberry and banana toppings.
8. 1 tsp vanilla essence Note: You can also add other assorted toppings, such as butter, confectioners sugar, honey, jams, preserves, sweetened whipped cream, or chocolate syrup.
9. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
10. 1 cup sliced bananas and strawberries
11. 1 cup maple syrup
